Rust Changelog - SEPTEMBER

SEPTEMBER 2023

Features

  • Added an attack helicopter specifically designed to decimate solo players for no reason (it can rapid fire rockets) - Because nothing says "welcome to Rust" like an insurmountable obstacle specifically engineered to ruin your day. Let's face it, solo players were having it too easy, right?
  • Added a homing missile launcher to combat these attack helicopters for the low price of several thousand manually farmed scrap - Good news! For those who have the time and patience to grind for hours on end, or perhaps have a masochistic streak, we've introduced a countermeasure that's almost as ridiculous as the problem it solves.
  • Added pay-to-win weapon racks targeted towards roaming clans for only $24.99 - Finally, the true essence of skill-based gameplay can be unlocked for less than the price of a decent meal. Your financial contribution allows you to bypass the very mechanics that make this game challenging, all while annoying the rest of the community. What's not to love?
  • Added the "Indigo" wire color option
